# YOUSELL Platform — Affiliate & Commission Revenue Strategy

## Research Date: 2026-03-12

---

## Executive Summary

YOUSELL's architecture touches 15+ paid services. By enrolling in affiliate/referral
programs for every service we use (and recommending them to clients we onboard),
we create a passive revenue layer on top of our core service fees. When we spin up
new Shopify stores for clients via automation, each store becomes a Shopify Partner
referral earning us **20% recurring commission** on their subscription.

### Estimated Annual Passive Revenue (at 50 active clients)

| Revenue Stream | Conservative Annual Estimate |
|---|---|
| Shopify Partner referrals | $13,800 – $27,600 |
| Tool referrals (Blotato, HeyGen, ElevenLabs, etc.) | $3,600 – $7,200 |
| Apify referrals (client scraping needs) | $1,200 – $3,000 |
| Hosting/infra referrals (Railway, Vercel/Netlify) | $600 – $1,800 |
| CJ Dropshipping order commissions | $2,400 – $6,000 |
| AliExpress affiliate commissions | $1,200 – $4,800 |
| **Total Passive Layer** | **$22,800 – $50,400** |

## TIER 1 — HIGH-VALUE PROGRAMS (Enroll Immediately)

### 1. Shopify Partner Program

**Status:** CRITICAL — this is the single highest-value referral in our stack.

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| Commission | **20% recurring** on merchant subscription fees |
| Duration | Ongoing (as long as merchant pays) |
| Shopify Plus referral | ~$460/month per merchant ($2,300 × 20%) |
| Standard plan referral | ~$8/month per Basic, ~$16/month per Shopify, ~$60/month per Advanced |
| POS Pro referral | $500 one-time + 20% recurring for 24 months |
| Markets Pro referral | 1% of GMV for 24 months (up to $50K per referral) |
| Cost to join | Free |
| Apply | https://www.shopify.com/partners |

**Why this matters:** If our automation platform spins up 10 Shopify stores/month on
the $79 Shopify plan, that's ~$190/month in recurring referral income, growing to
$2,280/year from just that cohort. At scale (100 stores), this becomes $22,800/year
in pure passive income — and it compounds as merchants upgrade plans.

**Shopify Affiliate Program** (separate from Partner):
- $150 one-time bounty per full-price plan referral
- 30-day cookie window
- Apply: https://www.shopify.com/affiliates
- Use this for content marketing / blog referrals to supplement Partner income

**Action Items:**
1. Register as Shopify Partner immediately
2. Build referral link into automated store provisioning workflow
3. Track each client store as a Partner referral
4. Register separately for the Shopify Affiliate Program for content/marketing use

## AFFILIATE LINK MANAGEMENT

### Recommended Tracking Setup

Use a centralized link management approach:

1. **Primary:** Create a subdomain (e.g., go.yousell.online) for all affiliate redirects
2. **Tracking:** Use UTM parameters for all links
3. **Dashboard:** Build an internal affiliate earnings tracker in the admin panel
4. **Compliance:** Maintain FTC disclosure on all client-facing materials

### Link Structure Example

```
go.yousell.online/shopify  → Shopify Partner referral link
go.yousell.online/apify    → Apify affiliate link
go.yousell.online/heygen   → HeyGen affiliate link
```

## KEY INSIGHTS

1. **Shopify Partner is the crown jewel** — 20% recurring with no cap and no expiration
   makes every automated store we spin up a perpetual revenue stream.

2. **The content tool stack (Blotato + HeyGen + ElevenLabs) has excellent affiliate
   programs** — bundling these into client packages creates natural referral flow.

3. **CJ Dropshipping's 2% seems small but scales with GMV** — a client doing $50K/month
   in orders generates $1,000/month in passive commission.

4. **Resend is the only critical service with zero affiliate opportunity** — supplement
   with marketing email tool referrals (ConvertKit/GetResponse) for that revenue gap.

5. **n8n at 30% is the highest SaaS affiliate rate in our stack** — if clients need
   automation, this is a strong recommendation to make.

6. **Supabase and Anthropic have no meaningful affiliate programs for our scale** —
   consider cloud provider partnerships (AWS/GCP) as an indirect path to commissions
   on AI/database spend.
